Why I Love Palawan

Palawan, often called the last frontier of the Philippines, is a paradise of breathtaking beaches, turquoise waters, and limestone cliffs. My trip was filled with island hopping in El Nido, exploring the Puerto Princesa Underground River, and relaxing in Coron’s hidden lagoons. Truly, it’s one of the most unforgettable destinations in the country.
