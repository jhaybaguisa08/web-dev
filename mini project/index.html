Security 3 min read Sep 2025

Passwords can be stolen when people trick you, when websites leak data, or when there are mistakes in software.

Simple explanation Like leaving a key under the mat:

If you reuse the same password everywhere and a website loses its list of passwords, bad people can try that same password on other sites. Phishing is when someone pretends to be your bank to trick you into typing your password on a fake page.

How to protect yourself

Use a password manager, turn on two-factor authentication, and don't click links in messages you don't expect.

A web framework is like a toolbox that helps developers build websites faster by providing common parts already made.

Simple explanation Imagine building a Lego city:

Instead of carving each Lego brick, a framework gives you ready-made pieces (like login pages, pages router, templates). Examples are React, Django, and Laravel.

What is 5G and will it make my phone faster?

Mobile 3 min read Jul 2025

5G is the newest generation of mobile internet. It can be a lot faster, but it depends on where you are and your phone.

Simple explanation Like upgrading from a small road to a highway:

5G can let more devices use more data at the same time and can reduce delays. But if you're far from a 5G tower or have an old phone, you might not feel the difference.

Real-world limits

Coverage, battery use, and whether apps actually need that much speed matter. Some 5G is built for long range, some for very high speeds in small areas.
